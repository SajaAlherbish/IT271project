Tourism

1. ALULA

Al Ula is renowned for its natural beauty, historic tombs, and contemporary urban architecture. Explore ancient sites like Dadan's timeless tombs and the UNESCO-listed Madain Saleh. Don't miss the modern marvel of Maraya Hall, nestled in Al Ula Valley.

2. BOULEVARD WORLD

Boulevard World offers its visitors the delight of exploring world civilizations and cultures, wandering through its fantastical facilities, and experiencing numerous exceptional adventures in each unique area. Enjoy exclusive privileges and magical experience throughout your visit when you go to Boulevard World.

3. SOUTHERN AREA

Asir is a region crossed by the Sarawat Mountains, stretching along the Red Sea coast from Jordan to Yemen. It features diverse landscapes, from green plateaus to vast deserts and towering rocky slopes. Its unique formations allow for experiencing three climates within just 100 kilometers. The Romans called it "Arabia Felix" due to its exceptionally refreshing environment.
