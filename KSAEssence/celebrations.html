National Celebrations

1. NATIONAL DAY

The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia celebrates its National Day, the day of the unification of the kingdom, on September 23rd of each year. This date goes back to the royal decree issued by the founding king, Abdulaziz, on the 17th of Jumada Al-Awwal, 1351 AH, which changed the name of the state from the Kingdom of Hejaz and Nejd and its Dependencies to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

2. FOUNDATION DAY

Saudi National Day, celebrated on February 22nd, marks the founding of the Saudi state. King Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud declared it an official holiday on January 27th, 2022. This day corresponds to historical events during the reign of Imam Muhammad bin Saud in Diriyah.

3. SAUDI FLAG DAY

Saudi Flag Day, officially the Flag Day, is a public holiday in Saudi Arabia observed annually on March 11 to commemorate the adoption of the unstandardized version of the country's national flag by King Abdulaziz ibn Saud in 1937.
