Vision 2030

1. NEOM

In 2017, Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman launched the NEOM project, led by the Public Investment Fund. It aims to turn the desert in the northwest of the Kingdom into a futuristic city operating solely on renewable energy, serving as a model for sustainable living and progress without harming the planet's health.

2. QIDDIYA

Al Qiddiya is a global hub for entertainment, sports, and culture. The city hosts several international sports arenas, concert halls, entertainment venues, sports and arts academies, and a race track. It offers visitors a variety of outdoor activities, adventures, and entertainment cities, including the Six Flags Entertainment City and the water park, providing visitors with unique games and experiences.

3. RED SEA GLOBAL

Stretching along the Kingdom's western coast, the destination covers 28,000 square kilometers, featuring pristine islands, clear beaches, and natural wonders like dormant volcanoes and stunning mountains. It's home to the world's fourth-largest thriving coral reef, offering unmatched marine habitats. Beyond tourism, the Red Sea destination holds undiscovered historical and cultural sites, rich in heritage.
